List: Troll Lines

by Nis published on 2021-09-14

Taxonomy of troll lines.

  • Cut-Off Troll Lines
    • "Genesis" Troll Line
    • Purposeless "Unenlightenment" Troll Line
    • "Retirement" Troll Line
  • Explicit Deathism
    • "Everything is like, society and stuff, there's no escaping it, so we need to destroy everything"
    • "We must end all suffering once and for all"
  • Explicit Evil
    • Just straight up saying they're evil, they believe in might makes right, etc. Common with vampires especially.
    • Pure Gaslighting
      • Contradiction Troll Line
      • "It's nobody's fault"
        • "hurt people hurt people"
      • "I'm making it better by confessing!"

Glossary: Qaf

by Nis published on 2021-09-09

Absolute grip on perfect action, not wasting a single moment. Can kill gods (by making the path of your victory itself a weapon). See also: Soul Touch.

A concept I first conceived in childhood. Makes sleeping difficult for me. I think most people generate some version of this concept in childhood. The concept of 'absoluteness' (in general) must come from within you, and is near to 'freedom'.

Named after "Qaf, the Heaven-Violating Spear", a yozi from Exalted:

You can ascend Qaf physically, but the only way to make real progress up his slopes is through spiritual discovery and understanding.

Glossary: Retirement Troll Line

by Nis published on 2021-09-03

Good people don't "retire". Equivalently, good people don't judge someone else as good, without retaining the ability to change their mind.

Examples:

MIRI wanted to have an artificial intelligence (i.e. a "perfect being") analyze humans' brains, to figure out what they want and then do that, calling it "value loading".

The trope where someone sees ascension as creating the "perfect human" or the "perfect being" to replace them (but oh no! it turns out to have cancer too!).

Only after Atreyu gives Bastian's name and promises to complete all the stories for him does the Water of Life allow Bastian to return to the human world
